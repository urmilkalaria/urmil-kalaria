Summer Internship at PES Innovation Labs

PES Innovation Lab (PIL) is a unique community which inculcates the spirit of student research. PIL offered summer internship opportunities to 2nd year students where selected student worked on a team based projects with mentors. I personally worked on project called PseudoDeploy, where me and my teammates built an emulator to deploy a Federated Learning model on k8s. It was a great learning experience where I learned about Distributed systems and worked on cloud technologies like k8s and docker. This internship helped me to connect with a great tech community with like minded bunch of alumini, seniors, and peers to guide, help, and collobrate at every possible step.
